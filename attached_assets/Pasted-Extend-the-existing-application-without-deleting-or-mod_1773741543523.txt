1. ROLE SYSTEM (FINAL STRUCTURE)

Ensure the following roles exist:

* admin
* director
* technical_director
* athletic_director
* secretary
* coach
* fitness_coach

Each user must have:

* role
* club_id

---

2. ROLE PERMISSIONS AND SIDEBAR

ADMIN:

* full access to all sections
* access billing

DIRECTOR:

* view teams, players, staff, reports
* no editing of training or payments

TECHNICAL DIRECTOR:

* manage teams and coaches
* view training programs
* view fitness programs (read-only)

ATHLETIC DIRECTOR:

* manage fitness programs
* view all players and teams
* view training sessions (read-only)

SECRETARY:

* manage players
* manage registrations
* manage payments
* manage documents
* manage equipment
* no access to training or tactical board

COACH:

* manage only assigned teams
* manage players in assigned teams
* create trainings
* use tactical board
* manage matches

FITNESS COACH:

* manage only assigned teams
* create fitness programs
* track player performance
* no access to tactics or payments

---

3. TEAM ASSIGNMENT

Ensure:

* coach is assigned to one or more teams
* fitness_coach is assigned to one or more teams

Restrict data access based on assigned teams.
