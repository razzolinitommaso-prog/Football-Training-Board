2. PERMISSIONS

ADMIN:

* full access to all data and settings

DIRECTOR:

* view teams, players, staff, reports
* cannot edit trainings or payments

TECHNICAL DIRECTOR:

* manage teams and coaches
* evaluate players
* cannot access payments

SECRETARY:

* manage players
* manage registrations
* manage documents
* manage payments
* manage equipment
* cannot access training or tactics

COACH:

* manage own teams only
* create trainings
* manage exercises
* use tactical board
* manage match call-ups
* cannot access payments or registrations

FITNESS COACH:

* manage fitness programs
* track player performance
* cannot access tactics or payments
